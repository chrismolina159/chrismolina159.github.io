Why computer science?

Ever since I was a kid, I've had a love for video games and have had a strong fascination with how things worked. After realizing that being a video game tester may not be the best career path for me, I broadened my horizons to technology in general. Flip phones were becoming less popular, computers were getting faster, and video games' graphics were consistently improving, and I wanted to know how. After taking my first computer science course in high school, I knew exactly what I wanted to study. While I do not know the specifics of how flip phones eventually became iPhones, I have learned some information about software and how things work under the hood, and I hope to continue growing this information in my future jobs.
